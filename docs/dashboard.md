# The Dashboard & Your Net Worth

The **Dashboard** is the heartbeat of your Firesheet. It transforms raw data from your bank into a visual overview of your financial health.

## Understanding Your Net Worth

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see your **Net Worth**. This is a single number that represents your total financial value. In Firesheet, this is composed of three main parts:

### 1. Liquid Assets (Cash)
This is the sum of all balances in your connected bank accounts (Checking, Savings, etc.). It represents money you can spend or move immediately.

### 2. Investments
This includes the value of your stocks, bonds, crypto, or pension funds defined in the [Investments](./investments) tab. Firesheet tracks the current value of these assets to give you a complete picture.

### 3. Liabilities (Debt)
This is money you owe, such as credit card balances, personal loans, or mortgages defined in the `debt` tab. Liabilities are subtracted from your assets to calculate your true Net Worth.

**Net Worth = (Cash + Investments) - Debt**

## Dashboard Widgets

- **Monthly Income vs. Expenses**: A bar chart showing if you saved money or overspent in previous months.
- **Spending by Category**: A pie chart showing which categories (like "Housing" or "Leisure") are consuming most of your budget.
- **Safe-to-Spend**: Based on your monthly budget and current spending, this tells you how much "fun money" you have left for the month.
